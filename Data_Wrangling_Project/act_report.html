Some of the insights observed are explained below:

  1. After doing and value counts to the dogs names we can observed that the three more used names are Lucy, Charlie and Cooper, with a frequency of 10 each one of them.
  2. The dogs breeds in the top three higher rating are the clumber, bouvier des flandres and pomeranian with ratings of 2.70, 1.30 and 1.25, respectively. In the oder hand, the worst ranked are the japanese spaniel, soft coated wheaten terrier and the scotch terrier and walker hound, with 0.50, 0.85, 0.90 and 0.90, respectively.
  3. The most retweeted dog breeds are the bedlington terrier, afghan hound and standard poodle with 7510, 5976 and 5508 retweets, respectively. And the less retweeted are the groenendael, brabancon griffon and japanese spaniel with 277, 288 and 471 retweets, respectively.
  4. The most posted breeds are golden retriever, labrador retriever and pembroke with 156, 106 and 94 post, respectively. Meanwhile the less posted were the bouvier des flandres, entlebucher, standard schnauzer, clumber, scotch terrier, japanese spaniel, silky terrier and irish wolfhound with 1 appearance each of them.

From these insights we can conclude that it could be a relation between the most rated breeds and the frequency that they are posted. Meanwhile, there is no clear relation between the dogs breed frequency and the retweets that they received.
